Volume Scanner: Quickstart Guide
The Volume Scanner tracks each pair and computes how much has been bought and sold.
We offer the stats of the last 5 and 15 minutes, and we are looking to add support for 30 and 60 minutes soon.

Here is a quick explanation of the meaning of each column on the table:
Bought: refers to the amount that has been bought.

Sold: refers to the amount that has been sold.

Total Traded: refers to the total amount that has been traded. This is basically a sum of the bought and the sold amount.

Diff: this column shows the net difference of the amounts traded. This is basically the amount bought minus the amount sold. A positive value indicates buying pressure, while a negative value implies selling pressure.

Total % 24HV: This value indicates how much of the total traded equals in percentage to the 24 Hours volume.

For example:
Total traded: 10 BTC
24H Volume: 100 BTC
Total % 24HV: 10% (Since the total traded in the selected timeframe equals to the 10% of the 24H volume)

Vol: The total amount traded in Bitcoin during the last 24H hours.
